Privacy Policy for Flow Journal

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Flow Journal ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by Gameanaut.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The Application is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

All data entered into the Application (including activity names, energy levels, engagement scores, and timestamps) is stored locally on your device using Android's internal storage (SharedPreferences). This data remains exclusively in your possession.

2. Third-Party Access

Since the data is stored locally on your device, we (Gameanaut) have no access to your information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties.

3. Permissions

The Application requires no special permissions (such as camera, location, or contacts) to function.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us via our website at gameanaut.com.
