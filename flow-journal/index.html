Flow Journal

A simple log for activities, energy, and engagement. Everything stays on your device.

Privacy policy

What it does

Flow Journal pairs short activity notes with energy and engagement scores. The goal is to notice what helps you feel better and what drains you without any extra noise.

Why it is simple

  • Quick entries with a title, note, and scores from -10 to +10.
  • No accounts, syncing, or ads.
  • Data is stored locally and can be reviewed anytime.

How to log

  • Write the activity and a short description.
  • Record how much energy it gave or took and how engaged you felt.
  • Look back over the list to spot patterns you want to repeat or avoid.
