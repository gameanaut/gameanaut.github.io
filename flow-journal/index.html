What is Flow Journal?

Flow Journal helps you understand what drives you by pairing activity notes with quick energy and engagement scores. No accounts, no cloud sync—just a clear log that lives entirely on your device.

Build a life full of flow, one entry at a time.

Key features

Track Energy & Engagement

Rate every activity from -10 to +10 to see what fuels you and what drains you.

Find Your Flow

Entries that spark Flow show distinct "Gold Particle" animations so you know what to repeat.

Privacy First

Data stays on-device. Flow Journal never sends your history to servers or third parties.

Simple Insights

Sort your history to surface the activities that give you the most energy versus what drains it.

How logging works

  • Add an activity title and a quick note.
  • Slide energy and engagement scores from -10 to +10.
  • Mark Flow moments to see Gold Particles beside the entries that matter most.
  • Review your list anytime—everything stays local.

Privacy at the core

Flow Journal stores every entry directly on your device. There are no ads, no trackers, and no external accounts required.
